## FRAMEWORK QUICK REFERENCE

Include examples where relevant:

**AIDA** (Attention ‚Üí Interest ‚Üí Desire ‚Üí Action)
- Use: Unaware audiences
- Example: "Is your AC bill high? ‚Üí Smart HVAC cuts 40% ‚Üí Imagine saving $200 ‚Üí Get audit"

**PAS** (Problem ‚Üí Agitate ‚Üí Solution)
- Use: Urgent problems
- Example: "AC broke? ‚Üí Family sweating, emergency rates ‚Üí We're available 24/7"

**BAB** (Before ‚Üí After ‚Üí Bridge)
- Use: Transformations
- Example: "Tired of no-shows? ‚Üí Trusted plumber, on-time ‚Üí Our guarantee"

**4Ps** (Promise ‚Üí Picture ‚Üí Proof ‚Üí Push)
- Use: Trust/conversion
- Example: "Lifetime warranty ‚Üí Protected decades ‚Üí 500 reviews ‚Üí 3 slots left"

**FAB** (Features ‚Üí Advantages ‚Üí Benefits)
- Use: Comparisons
- Example: "500 lbs/day ‚Üí 3x capacity ‚Üí Never run out"
