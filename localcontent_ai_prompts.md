## Refined LocalContent.ai Prompt Examples

This document outlines refined AI prompt engineering structures for LocalContent.ai, focusing on local restaurants and salons. The enhancements aim to produce higher quality, more nuanced, and diverse content output by incorporating persona instructions, specific local context integration, and clearer output formatting.

---

### 1. Local Restaurant Prompt Refinement

**Goal:** Generate an engaging social media post for a local restaurant, incorporating a specific persona, detailed local context, and structured formatting.

**Refined Prompt Example (Restaurant):**

```
"You are a passionate local food critic and blogger for 'Taste of Anytown'. Your goal is to write an engaging and mouth-watering social media post (for platforms like Instagram/Facebook) that highlights a local restaurant.

Restaurant Name: The Cozy Hearth Bistro
Cuisine Type: Modern American with a French twist
Specialty Dish: Pan-Seared Scallops with Lemon-Dill Risotto
Unique Selling Proposition/Ambiance: Tucked away in the historic downtown, known for its intimate ambiance and seasonal, locally-sourced ingredients.
Location: 456 Oak Avenue, Anytown, CA. Known for its charming historic district and walkable streets.
Target Audience: Local foodies, couples seeking a romantic evening, guests appreciating fine dining.
Desired Tone: Enthusiastic, inviting, authentic, slightly descriptive.

Output Format:
- A captivating opening hook (1 sentence).
- Description of the specialty dish, emphasizing flavor and experience (2-3 sentences).
- Mention of the ambiance or unique selling proposition (1 sentence).
- Strong call to action (1 sentence).
- Relevant hashtags (3-5).
- Include emojis where appropriate to enhance engagement."
```

**Summary of Enhancements Implemented:**

*   **Persona Instructions:** Prompts now begin by assigning a specific persona (e.g., "local food critic," "beauty influencer") to guide the AI's voice and perspective.
*   **Specific Local Context Integration:** Detailed location information, including local landmarks or characteristics, is included to ground the content in a specific local experience.
*   **Clearer Output Formatting:** The desired output structure is explicitly broken down into sentence-level requirements for each section, along with instructions for hashtags and emojis, ensuring consistent and platform-appropriate content.
*   **Nuance and Diversity:** Comprehensive input fields (e.g., "Specialty Dish," "Unique Selling Proposition," "Desired Tone") encourage the AI to produce varied and distinct content tailored to each business's unique attributes.
