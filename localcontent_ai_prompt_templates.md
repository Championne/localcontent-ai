# LocalContent.ai Prompt Templates for Service Businesses

This document outlines refined AI prompt templates for two service-based local business industries: Electricians and HVAC Specialists. Each template includes detailed guidelines on persona, local context integration, output formatting, an example prompt, and hypothetical generated content.

---

## 1. Electricians: Website Service Description for a Common Electrical Issue Repair

**Scenario:** Generating a website service description for a common electrical problem like "Flickering Lights Repair."

### Guidelines:

*   **Persona:**
    *   **Tone:** Professional, trustworthy, knowledgeable, slightly empathetic, clear, and concise.
    *   **Voice:** An expert electrician dedicated to safety and providing reliable, long-lasting solutions.
    *   **Goal:** To educate potential customers about the issue, alleviate concerns, and position the business as the go-to local expert.

*   **Specific Local Context Integration:**
    *   **City/Town Name:** Always integrate the specific city or town where the service is offered (e.g., "serving Springfield, IL").
    *   **Neighborhoods/Areas (Optional):** Mention specific local neighborhoods or surrounding service areas if relevant to emphasize hyper-local reach.
    *   **Local Regulations/Codes (Implied):** Without explicitly stating "we follow [city] codes," the content should subtly convey adherence to local electrical standards through phrases like "safe, code-compliant solutions."
    *   **Common Local Issues (Optional):** If there are electrical problems frequently encountered in the specified locale due to climate, infrastructure age, etc., these can be hinted at if the prompt allows.

*   **Clear Output Formatting:**
    *   **Main Title (H1/H2):** Clear, keyword-rich service title.
    *   **Introduction (1-2 paragraphs):** Briefly describe the problem and its potential impact on daily life and safety.
    *   **Symptoms / When to Call (Bulleted List):** An easy-to-read list of signs that indicate the issue, helping customers self-diagnose when to seek professional help.
    *   **Our Solution (1-2 paragraphs):** Explain the professional service provided, detailing the process, and highlighting expertise, safety protocols, and efficient problem resolution.
    *   **Why Choose Us (1 paragraph or short bullet points):** Briefly emphasize local expertise, licensed and insured professionals, prompt service, and customer satisfaction.
    *   **Call to Action (CTA):** A clear, concise instruction to book a service or request a quote, including local contact information (phone number, link).

### Example Prompt:

```
"Generate a website service description for 'Flickering Lights Repair' for an electrician business operating in 'Springfield, IL'. The content should adopt a professional and reassuring persona, emphasizing safety and local expertise. Include a bulleted list of common causes/symptoms and a clear call to action, providing a placeholder for the phone number."
```

## 2. HVAC Specialists: Social Media Post Promoting Seasonal Maintenance Checks

**Scenario:** Creating a social media post (e.g., for Facebook or Instagram) to encourage "Fall HVAC Maintenance."

### Guidelines:

*   **Persona:**
    *   **Tone:** Friendly, helpful, informative, slightly urgent (for timely maintenance), proactive, and community-oriented.
    *   **Voice:** A trusted local HVAC expert emphasizing comfort, energy savings, health, and preventative care.
    *   **Goal:** To engage local residents, highlight the benefits of seasonal maintenance, and drive inquiries/bookings for service.

*   **Specific Local Context Integration:**
    *   **City/Town Name:** Explicitly mention the local city or town to immediately connect with the audience (e.g., "For our neighbors in Portland, ME").
    *   **Local Weather/Season:** Refer to specific local weather conditions or upcoming seasonal changes (e.g., "As Portland, ME heads into brisk autumn," or "before the Maine winter truly bites") to make the maintenance advice highly relevant and time-sensitive.
    *   **Geographic References (Optional):** Can briefly reference local landmarks or
        specific challenges common to the area's climate (e.g., "battling those damp New England chills").

*   **Clear Output Formatting:**
    *   **Catchy Headline/Hook (1 sentence):** Grab attention immediately.
    *   **Problem/Benefit Statement (1-2 sentences):** Briefly state why maintenance is vital, tying it directly to local seasonal needs (e.g., preparing for cold, saving on heating bills).
    *   **Key Benefits (Bulleted List or Emoji-rich Short Phrases):** An easy-to-digest list of advantages (e.g., energy savings, improved air quality, extended unit life). Emojis enhance readability and engagement on social media.
    *   **Special Offer / Call to Action (1-2 sentences):** A clear instruction on how to book, including any urgency (e.g., "limited-time offer," "book before winter").
    *   **Relevant Hashtags:** A mix of local, industry-specific, and benefit-oriented hashtags for discoverability.
    *   **Visual Suggestion (Optional but Recommended):** Hint at the type of image that would accompany the post.

### Example Prompt:

```
"Create an engaging social media post (e.g., Facebook/Instagram caption) encouraging 'Fall HVAC Maintenance' for an HVAC specialist business in 'Portland, ME'. The post should be friendly, highlight energy savings and comfort during the upcoming colder months, and include a limited-time special offer. Use relevant emojis and hashtags, and suggest an accompanying image."
```
