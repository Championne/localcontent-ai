## Group Tiering

### Tier 1: Top 10-15 Groups (High Value)
- Highest member count + most active
- Personal touch approach
- 2-3 posts per week max
- Reply to every comment
- These are your best prospects - don't burn them

### Tier 2: Medium Groups (Scale)
- 1,000-10,000 members
- Less admin scrutiny
- 1 post per day across different groups
- Less membership overlap

### Tier 3: Smaller Groups (Batch)
- Under 1,000 members
- Lower risk, lower reward
- Can move faster here

- **Never same content in same group**
- **Vary wording between groups**

## Engagement Rules

### When Someone Comments Positively
> "Glad it helped! Let me know if you have any questions about implementing it."

### When Someone Asks a Question
> "Great question! [Give helpful answer]. Happy to chat more if you want to DM me."

### When Someone DMs After Downloading
> "Hey! Glad you grabbed the guide. How's your review situation looking right now? Happy to help if you're stuck on anything."

**Then naturally transition (only if they engage):**
> "By the way, I also use this tool called GeoSpark for my social media and Google posts - saves me a ton of time. Happy to show you if you're interested."
