## Factors That Improve Results

- **Better targeting**: Verified emails, correct industry, decision makers
- **Strong value prop**: Clear benefit in subject + first line
- **Quick response**: Reply to interested leads within 5 minutes
- **Good demos**: Focus on their problems, not features
- **Product-market fit**: Solve a real pain point

## Factors That Hurt Results

- Poor email copy (too salesy, too long)
- Wrong audience (bad targeting)
- Slow response time (>24 hours)
- High bounce rates (bad data quality)
- Aggressive/spammy tone
- No follow-up sequence

Instead of employees, use commission-based partners:

- 20-30% commission on closed deals
- They bring market expertise
- Easy to scale up/down
