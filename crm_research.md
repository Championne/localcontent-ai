### **CRM Systems for a 3-Person Sales Team**

This research focuses on simple, scalable, and cost-effective CRM systems suitable for a small sales team (3 people), specifically considering their ability to track leads, manage customer interactions across email, social media, and phone calls, and provide transparent reporting for sales performance and commissions.

---

### **1. Zoho CRM (Recommended)**

*   **Pros:**
    *   **Comprehensive Features:** Offers robust contact management, lead tracking, sales pipeline management, and customizable modules.
    *   **Cost-Effective:** Has a generous free tier for up to 3 users and affordable paid plans (Standard Edition) that provide more features.
    *   **Scalable:** Grows with your team and business needs.
    *   **Integrations:** Excellent integration with other Zoho products (Zoho Mail, Zoho Marketing Automation, Zoho Analytics) and third-party apps, including major phone systems and social media platforms.
    *   **Commission Management (via Extension/Feature):** Zoho CRM offers a "Commission Management" feature or extension which allows setting up commission rules, tracking sales performance, and calculating commissions effectively. This directly addresses your transparency requirement for earnings.
    *   **Multi-channel:** Supports tracking interactions across email, social media, and phone calls.
*   **Cons:**
    *   Can have a slight learning curve due to its extensive features, but generally intuitive.
    *   The free plan, while generous, will have limitations in advanced reporting or integrations.
*   **Estimated Costs (for 3 users):**
    *   **Free Edition:** (Up to 3 users) Basic features, good for starting.
    *   **Standard Edition:** ~$14-20/user/month (billed annually) - Recommended for balanced features including reporting and basic integrations. For 3 users: **$42 - $60/month.**

---

### **2. HubSpot CRM**

*   **Pros:**
    *   **User-Friendly:** Highly intuitive interface, often praised for ease of use.
    *   **Strong Free Tier:** Comprehensive free CRM with contact management, deal pipelines, and basic reporting.
    *   **Marketing & Sales Hub Integration:** Seamless integration with HubSpot's vast array of marketing and sales tools.
    *   **Multi-channel:** Good for tracking interactions across email and social media.
*   **Cons:**
    *   **Commission Tracking:** Native commission tracking built into HubSpot's CRM Free or Starter tiers is limited or non-existent. You would likely need to rely on integrations with third-party commission tracking software or manual tracking, which adds complexity and cost.
    *   **Scalability Cost:** Becomes significantly more expensive as you upgrade to paid Sales Hub or Marketing Hub tiers to unlock advanced features.
*   **Estimated Costs (for 3 users):**
    *   **Free CRM:** Free.
    *   **Sales Hub Starter:** ~$20-30/user/month (billed annually). For 3 users: **$60 - $90/month.**

---

### **3. Monday CRM**

*   **Pros:**
    *   **Highly Customizable:** Adaptable interface using boards and workflows; very visual.
    *   **Integrations:** Over 100 app integrations, including e-signature tools and business phone systems.
    *   **Automation:** Can automate lead assignment and notifications for email opens.
    *   **Flexible Templates:** Good for managing pipelines and customer data.
*   **Cons:**
    *   **Not a built-for-purpose CRM:** While flexible, it's originally a project management tool adapted for CRM, which might mean some native CRM functionalities aren't as robust as dedicated CRMs.
    *   **Commission Tracking:** Similar to HubSpot, native commission tracking is not a primary feature and would likely require custom setup or integrations, potentially adding complexity.
    *   **Cost:** Pricing can add up quickly depending on user count and features needed.
*   **Estimated Costs (for 3 users):**
    *   **Basic CRM:** ~$10-15/user/month (billed annually). For 3 users: **$30 - $45/month.**
    *   **Standard CRM:** ~$14-20/user/month (billed annually) for more features. For 3 users: **$42 - $60/month.**

---

### **Recommendation for GeoSpark:**

**Zoho CRM (Standard Edition)** remains the strongest recommendation. It provides the best balance of essential sales features, cost-effectiveness, strong integration capabilities, and crucially, a native or easily implementable *commission management* feature. This directly addresses your need for transparent commission tracking for your sales team.
