## 6. The Indispensability Framework

### Stickiness Levels

| Level | Description | Features |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Useful** | They could leave | Good content, saves time |
| **Valuable** | Inconvenient to leave | Historical data, custom templates |
| **Critical** | Painful to leave | Auto-review responses, integrations |
| **Indispensable** | Can't imagine without | AI knows their voice, competitor intel |

### Tactics for Indispensability

1. **Accumulating Value** - "You've generated 2,847 posts. AI learned 847 preferences."
2. **Social Proof Lock-in** - Display their reviews/testimonials in dashboard
3. **Competitor Fear** - Weekly: "Competitor posted 12 times. You posted 8."
4. **Network Effects** - "3 businesses nearby use LocalContent.ai - cross-promote?"
5. **Seasonal Predictions** - "Slow period in 3 weeks. Here's your pre-built campaign."
6. **Customer Intelligence** - Store review insights; leaving = losing knowledge
7. **Impact Dashboard** - Clear ROI proof they'd lose access to

## 7. Impact Analytics Dashboard (NEW - CRITICAL)

### Why This Is P0 Priority

**Churn reason #1:** "I don't know if it's working"

The Impact Analytics Dashboard transforms LocalContent.ai from "a content tool" to "the scoreboard for my business growth."

### Core Components

1. **Baseline Snapshot** - Capture metrics at signup (GMB views, rankings, reviews)
2. **Before/After Comparison** - Clear visual of improvement
3. **Growth Trend Charts** - Momentum visualization
4. **Shareable Reports** - PDF/social sharing for bragging
5. **Competitive Benchmark** - "You're outperforming 78% of plumbers in Austin"
6. **"What If You Stop" Calculator** - Loss aversion churn prevention

### Key Metrics to Track

| Category | Metrics |
|----------|---------|
| **Visibility** | GMB views, search impressions, rankings, Local Pack appearances |
| **Engagement** | GMB actions, website sessions, social engagement, CTR |
| **Reputation** | Reviews, ratings, review velocity, response rate, sentiment |
| **Conversion** | Phone calls, direction requests, form submissions, bookings |
