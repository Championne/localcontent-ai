## The Content Consistency Crisis: A Small Business Reality

Small business owners wear many hats. From managing operations and serving customers to handling finances, marketing often takes a backseat. This leads to:

*   **Scattered Efforts:** Inconsistent posting schedules confuse algorithms and your audience.
*   **Writer's Block:** Constantly coming up with fresh, relevant ideas is exhausting.
*   **Resource Drain:** Hiring dedicated content creators can be expensive.
*   **Brand Dilution:** Without a cohesive strategy, your message can become fragmented across platforms.

These challenges collectively contribute to the **content consistency crisis**, making it incredibly difficult for local businesses to build authority and engage with their community effectively.
