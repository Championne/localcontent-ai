## üîë FRAMEWORK QUICK REFERENCE

Include these where relevant:

**AIDA** (Attention ‚Üí Interest ‚Üí Desire ‚Üí Action)
- **Use:** Unaware audiences, awareness campaigns
- **Example:** "Is your AC bill high? ‚Üí Smart HVAC cuts 40% ‚Üí Imagine $200 savings ‚Üí Get audit"

**PAS** (Problem ‚Üí Agitate ‚Üí Solution)
- **Use:** Urgent problems, high pain awareness
- **Example:** "AC broke? ‚Üí Family sweating, emergency rates ‚Üí We're available 24/7"

**BAB** (Before ‚Üí After ‚Üí Bridge)
- **Use:** Transformations, service businesses
- **Example:** "Tired of no-shows? ‚Üí Trusted on-time plumber ‚Üí Our guarantee delivers"

**4Ps** (Promise ‚Üí Picture ‚Üí Proof ‚Üí Push)
- **Use:** Trust needed, final conversion
- **Example:** "Lifetime warranty ‚Üí Protected decades ‚Üí 500 reviews ‚Üí 3 slots left"

**FAB** (Features ‚Üí Advantages ‚Üí Benefits)
- **Use:** Comparisons, technical buyers
- **Example:** "500 lbs/day ‚Üí 3x capacity ‚Üí Never run out during rush"
