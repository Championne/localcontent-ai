# LocalContent.ai — Local Market Research

## Purpose
Determine which geographic markets to target first and understand how local businesses differ by region.

---

## 1. Market Selection Criteria

### Prioritization Scorecard

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Market Size** | 25% | Number of local businesses, SMB density |
| **Digital Maturity** | 20% | % using digital marketing, content adoption |
| **Competition** | 15% | Existing local content tools, market saturation |
| **Language Fit** | 15% | English-first easier, translation complexity |
| **Willingness to Pay** | 15% | SMB marketing budgets, SaaS adoption |
| **Ease of Entry** | 10% | Payment methods, regulations, support needs |

### Scoring Scale
- 5 = Excellent fit
- 4 = Good fit
- 3 = Moderate fit
- 2 = Challenging
- 1 = Poor fit

## 3. Research Questions by Market

### Market Size & Opportunity
- [ ] How many local businesses exist?
- [ ] What % are actively doing digital marketing?
- [ ] What's the annual growth rate of SMBs?
- [ ] Which industries dominate? (Restaurants, services, retail)

### Digital Behavior
- [ ] What platforms do local businesses use? (Google, Facebook, Instagram, local alternatives)
- [ ] What content formats are common? (Blog, social, video)
- [ ] How do they currently create content? (DIY, agency, nothing)
- [ ] What's their content frequency expectation?

### Competition Landscape
- [ ] What AI writing tools are popular in this market?
- [ ] Are there local competitors we don't know about?
- [ ] What's the pricing of existing solutions?
- [ ] What gaps exist that competitors don't fill?

### Pricing & Payment
- [ ] What do SMBs typically pay for marketing tools? ($/€/£ range)
- [ ] What payment methods are preferred? (Credit card, invoice, local methods)
- [ ] Is annual vs monthly billing preferred?
- [ ] What's the price sensitivity level?

### Language & Localization
- [ ] Is English acceptable or must content be in local language?
- [ ] Are there dialects/regional variations? (US vs UK English, Latin American vs Spain Spanish)
- [ ] What cultural factors affect content tone?
- [ ] Are there local SEO considerations? (Yandex in Russia, Baidu in China)

### Regulatory & Trust
- [ ] What data privacy regulations apply? (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- [ ] Are there industry-specific regulations? (Healthcare, finance)
- [ ] What builds trust with local SMBs? (Local support, testimonials, certifications)
- [ ] What payment/invoice requirements exist?

## 6. Data Sources for Research

### Market Size Data
- Eurostat (EU business statistics)
- US Census Bureau (County Business Patterns)
- UK Office for National Statistics
- Statista (paid, comprehensive)
- World Bank SME data

### Digital Behavior
- Google Trends (by country)
- SimilarWeb (competitor traffic by region)
- DataReportal (Digital reports by country)
- Social media platform statistics

### Competition
- G2.com (filter by region)
- Capterra (localized versions)
- Product Hunt (launch locations)
- Local SaaS directories

### Pricing Research
- Competitor websites (check localized pricing)
- Currency conversion and PPP adjustments
- Local SaaS pricing surveys

## 7. Recommended Initial Focus

### Phase 1: English-Speaking Markets
**Primary:** United States, United Kingdom
**Secondary:** Canada, Australia

**Rationale:**
- No translation needed initially
- Largest addressable market
- Most data available for research
- Established SaaS buying behavior

### Phase 2: High-English-Proficiency EU
**Primary:** Netherlands, Ireland
**Secondary:** Germany (with German content)

**Rationale:**
- Can launch with English, add local language
- GDPR-compliant from day 1
- Strong SMB tech adoption

### Phase 3: Expand Based on Learnings
- Evaluate Spanish market (Spain + LATAM)
- Evaluate French market (France + Canada)
- Consider APAC (Singapore, Australia expansion)
