## Analysis Plan

After 50+ responses:

1. **Segment by industry** - Which has highest pain?
2. **Identify top pain points** - What do they struggle with most?
3. **Price sensitivity** - What can they afford?
4. **Feature priorities** - What do they want most?
5. **Language patterns** - How do they describe problems?

Export to: `/localcontent_ai/research/survey-results/analysis.md`
