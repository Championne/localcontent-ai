# Competition Intelligence Module for LocalContent.ai

## 1. Types of Competitive Data to Collect

This module will focus on gathering a comprehensive array of data points to provide a holistic view of competitors' online strategies. Data collection will primarily focus on publicly available information and estimated metrics.

### 1.1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Data
*   **Competitor Keywords**: Top ranking keywords, branded vs. non-branded keywords, keyword difficulty, search volume.
*   **Content Strategies**: Top-performing content (articles, blogs, local guides), content length, readability, topics covered, content freshness, backlink profiles to competitor content.
*   **Website Structure**: URL structure, site speed, mobile-friendliness, internal linking, schema markup usage.
*   **Local SEO Signals**: Google My Business (GMB) profiles, local citations, reviews and ratings, local landing page optimization.

### 1.2. Paid Advertising (PPC) Data
*   **Ad Campaigns**: Active ad copies, landing pages, ad historical data, ad spend estimates, geographic targeting.
*   **Keyword Bidding**: Keywords competitors are bidding on, estimated cost-per-click (CPC), ad positions.
*   **Display Advertising**: Ad creatives, networks used, publisher placements.

### 1.3. Social Media Presence
*   **Engagement Metrics**: Follower growth, engagement rates (likes, shares, comments) across key platforms (Facebook, Instagram, X, LinkedIn).
*   **Content Types**: Most popular post formats (images, videos, text), topics discussed, posting frequency.
*   **Audience Demographics**: Estimated audience insights where available.

### 1.4. Website & Traffic Data
*   **Estimated Website Traffic**: Overall traffic volume, traffic sources (organic, direct, referral, social, paid).
*   **Top Pages/Content**: Pages attracting the most traffic and engagement.
*   **User Experience (UX) Signals**: Bounce rate estimates, time on site estimates.

### 1.5. Review & Reputation Management
*   **Review Platforms**: Reviews and ratings on Google, Yelp, industry-specific review sites.
*   **Sentiment Analysis**: Overall sentiment of reviews, common themes in positive and negative feedback.
*   **Competitor Responses**: How competitors manage and respond to reviews.

## 2. Proposed Insights to Provide

The collected data will be processed and analyzed to generate actionable insights, helping LocalContent.ai users refine their local content and marketing strategies.

### 2.1. Content Strategy Insights
*   **Content Gaps**: Identify topics, keywords, or content formats that competitors are ranking for but the user is not, or where their content is weaker.
*   **Keyword Opportunities**: Uncover high-volume, low-competition keywords overlooked by competitors.
*   **Top-Performing Content Analysis**: Breakdown of competitor content that drives the most traffic/engagement, with recommendations for similar content creation.
*   **Content Freshness Alerts**: Notify users when competitors publish new, high-ranking content in their target areas.

### 2.2. SEO Performance Insights
*   **SERP Feature Opportunities**: Identify opportunities to rank for local packs, featured snippets, people also ask, etc.
*   **Backlink Gap Analysis**: Suggest websites linking to competitors but not the user.
*   **Local Ranking Factors**: Highlight areas where competitors excel in local SEO (e.g., more reviews, better GMB optimization).

### 2.3. Advertising & PPC Insights
*   **Ad Spend Distribution Estimates**: Estimated budget allocation across different ad platforms and campaigns.
*   **Ad Copy & Landing Page Effectiveness**: Analysis of competitor ad creatives and landing pages, suggesting improvements for user campaigns.
*   **Geographic Targeting Gaps**: Identify regions where competitors are active with ads, but the user is not.

### 2.4. Social Media Insights
*   **Engagement Benchmarking**: Compare social media engagement rates against competitors.
*   **Audience Overlap**: Identify potential shared audiences and opportunities for cross-promotion or targeted content.
*   **Trending Topics**: Discover social media topics where competitors are gaining traction.

### 2.5. Reputation Insights
*   **Sentiment Comparison**: Compare review sentiment and common themes with competitors.
*   **Review Management Best Practices**: Analyze competitor responses to reviews and provide recommendations.

## 3. High-Level UI/Reporting Interface

The Competition Intelligence module will feature an intuitive and visually rich interface designed for easy data consumption and actionable insights.

### 3.1. Dashboard Overview
*   **Key Metrics Summary**: At-a-glance view of overall competitive landscape including total competitors tracked, average traffic, average keyword count, and prominent content themes.
*   **Performance Trends**: Graphs showing competitive performance over time for key metrics (e.g., organic traffic, ad spend, social engagement).
*   **Alerts & Notifications**: Highlight significant competitor activities (e.g., new ad campaigns, viral content, major ranking changes).

### 3.2. Competitor Profiles
*   **Individual Competitor Cards**: Each tracked competitor will have a dedicated card with their logo, website, and a summary of their performance.
*   **Detailed View**: Clicking on a competitor card will open a detailed profile page with:
    *   **Overview**: Summary of their online presence, market share estimates.
    *   **SEO Section**: Top keywords, ranking pages, backlink profile analysis, local SEO signals.
    *   **PPC Section**: Active ads, ad spend estimates, targeted keywords, landing pages.
    *   **Social Media Section**: Key platforms, engagement metrics, top posts, audience insights.
    *   **Content Section**: List of top-performing content, content topics, and freshness.
    *   **Reviews Section**: Sentiment analysis, review trends, and common themes.

### 3.3. Insights & Opportunities Section
*   **Content Gaps**: A dynamic list of content topics and keywords where the user can outperform competitors.
*   **Keyword Opportunities**: Suggested keywords with estimated search volume and difficulty, and competitor ranking data.
*   **Ad Campaign Recommendations**: Specific suggestions for ad copy, targeting, or budget adjustments based on competitor analysis.
*   **Local SEO Recommendations**: Actionable steps to improve local search visibility based on competitor performance.
*   **Social Media Strategy**: Ideas for engaging content and growth strategies based on competitor successes.

### 3.4. Reporting & Export
*   **Customizable Reports**: Users can generate reports based on specific metrics, timeframes, and competitor sets.
*   **Export Options**: Data and reports can be exported in various formats (CSV, PDF, image).
*   **Scheduled Reports**: Option to schedule regular reports to be delivered via email or within the platform.

### 3.5. Settings & Management
*   **Competitor Management**: Add, edit, or remove competitors to track.
*   **Alert Preferences**: Configure notification settings for competitor activities.
*   **Data Sources**: Information on data sources and update frequency.

This module aims to empower LocalContent.ai users with the necessary intelligence to outmaneuver their competition in the local online landscape.
